Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2024

1. Information We Collect

When you play Moto X3M games, we may collect:

  • Game progress and scores
  • Device information (browser type, device type)
  • IP address and general location
  • Game preferences and settings

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and improve our games
  • Save your game progress
  • Analyze game performance and user experience
  • Fix bugs and technical issues

3. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access or disclosure.

4. Children's Privacy

Our games are suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent.

5. Cookies

We use cookies to enhance your gaming experience and save your progress. You can control cookie settings through your browser.

6. Third-Party Services

Our games may contain links to third-party websites or services. We are not responsible for their privacy practices.

7. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page.

8. Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us through the contact form on our website.
