Everything You Need to Know About Moto X3M

Use the arrow keys to control your bike: Up Arrow to accelerate, Down Arrow to brake/reverse, Left/Right Arrows to rotate in the air. Press Space to restart the level. Master these controls to perform amazing stunts and complete levels faster!

Moto X3M has several exciting versions: Classic Moto X3M, Moto X3M Pool Party (summer theme), Moto X3M Spooky Land (Halloween theme), Moto X3M Winter (snow theme), and Moto X3M 2. Each version offers unique levels, obstacles, and environments.

To earn three stars, you need to complete the level as quickly as possible. Perform front flips and back flips while in the air to reduce your time. Each flip subtracts time from your final score. The faster you finish with more stunts, the more stars you'll earn!
